###### ABOUT

### Who we are

Since our inception we have been at the forefront of product design and innovation with many industry designs having come from our manufacturing facility.

This has established our brand as the premium supplier of quality caskets, equipment and requisites to the funeral industry, both in South Africa and within the African continent.

###### COMPANY OVERVIEW

### A quality conscious organisation

As a quality conscious organisation, we are committed to providing bereaved families with products that are manufactured with a strict and consistent commitment to the highest quality standards.

Our customer network is vast, containing large corporates as well as SMEs, and is serviced by an extensive branch (hub) network that allows our products to be delivered quickly & efficiently throughout South Africa, our neighbouring countries, the African continent as well as abroad.

### [Read more](https://www.safuneral.co.za/about/\#)

We will continue to invest in world-class machinery, production line efficiencies, and technological innovations that will allow our manufacturing facility to offer new, exciting and innovative products so that our customers may increase their product offerings.

We intend to generate sufficient profits to finance continued growth in our chosen markets by focusing on operational excellence, the manufacture, and delivery of high-quality products, innovation, and sustainable growth models.
